About

I hail from Kashmir, the valley of saints. I am a PhD student at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. My research focus lies in the exciting field of graph machine learning, with a specific emphasis on explainability in graph ML. I am passionate about exploring methods and techniques that enhance our understanding of how graph models make predictions and decisions.

I completed my schooling at Tyndale Biscoe and pursued my bachelor's degree in Computer Science at NIT Srinagar. As a visiting student, I had the opportunity to join IIT Delhi in the fall of 2021, where I completed my final year of undergraduate studies. Following that, I continued my academic journey at IIT Delhi by enrolling in the PhD program.
